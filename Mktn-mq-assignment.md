You're building your marketing strategy by researching the market you want to enter. This assignment focuses on understanding the industry, competitors, and target customers through systematic research.

**Why This Matters**

Customer feedback alone isn't enough. You need to understand: How big is the market? Who are your competitors? What trends affect customers? Which customer segments should you target? This research ensures your marketing strategy is based on real market conditions.

## What You'll Create

A "Market Research Foundation" page on your Google Sites portfolio with four sections:

### **Section 1: Industry Analysis**

**What to research:**

* Market size (use multiple credible sources)  
* Is the market growing or shrinking?  
* Key trends affecting the industry (technology, regulations, customer behavior)  
* Total Addressable Market (TAM) \- explain how you calculated it

**Key questions:**

* How large is this market opportunity?  
* What's changing in this industry?  
* How do these changes affect your business idea?

**Include:** A visual showing market size, trends, or key forces

---

### **Section 2: Competitive Analysis**

**What to research:**

* Direct competitors (similar solutions)  
* Indirect competitors (alternative ways customers solve this problem)  
* Compare competitors: features, prices, strengths, weaknesses  
* Where competitors are strong and where they have gaps

**Key questions:**

* Who am I competing against?  
* How do competitors position themselves?  
* Where can I be different in ways that matter?

**Include:** A visual like a comparison chart or competitive positioning map

---

### **Section 3: Target Market**

**What to identify:**

* Different customer segments in your market  
* Which segments you'll focus on first and why  
* Details about your target customers (demographics, behaviors, motivations)  
* How large these segments are and how to reach them

**Key questions:**

* Which customer groups have the strongest need for my solution?  
* Which segments can I actually reach with my resources?  
* Why focus on these segments instead of others?

**Include:** A visual showing customer segments or target customer profiles

---

### **Section 4: Research Sources & Limitations**

**What to document:**

* List all sources you used  
* How you decided which sources were reliable  
* What data you couldn't find (research gaps)  
* What assumptions you had to make

**Be honest about:** What you know for certain versus what you're estimating

---

## Deliverable

One complete Google Sites page with all four sections, each including written analysis and a visual element.
