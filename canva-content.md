### Market Gaps

**Where It's Overcrowded:**
- Basic scheduling + dispatch — table stakes, many vendors
- Mobile job sheets + invoicing — commoditized
- Generic CRM features — no differentiation

**Gaps We Can Fill:**
- **AI-enabled operational decisions** — predictive routing, skill matching, pricing engines
- **Consultative implementation** — SMBs buy software but don't realize value
- **Flexible pricing for 10-50 employee shops** — too expensive or too basic

---

### Strategic Lessons (callout box)

1. **Differentiate on outcomes, not features** — lead with operational metrics improvements (% admin hours reduced), not "AI inside."

2. **Offer implementation + change management** — tailored onboarding creates stickiness beyond software adoption.

3. **Flexible value-based pricing** — tie to realized ROI rather than per-user fees that SMBs resist.

---

### Research Gaps (use as warning/alert cards)

1. **SMB counts by firm size not segmented**
   We know there are ~146K HVAC businesses, but breakdown by 10-50 employee range isn't publicly available. Our 45K estimate is extrapolated from general small business size distributions.

2. **Actual tech spend per SMB unverified**
   The $5K-$15K annual tech budget is estimated based on FSM software pricing tiers and industry articles, not primary survey data from contractors.

3. **Competitor market share not disclosed**
   ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, etc. don't publish customer counts or revenue. Competitive presence is inferred from review volume, forum mentions, and industry visibility.

4. **Regional variation not analyzed**
   Market size is national; we haven't broken down demand by region, climate zone, or urban vs. rural — factors that likely affect service business density and tech adoption.
